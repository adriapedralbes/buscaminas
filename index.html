How to Play

  • Left-click on a square to reveal it.
  • Right-click on a square to flag it as a mine.
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that square.
  • Clear all non-mine squares to win!

Keyboard Controls

  • Use Tab to navigate between cells
  • Press Enter or Space to reveal a cell
  • Press F to flag/unflag a cell
